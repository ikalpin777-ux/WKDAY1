Frequently Asked Questions
What is Workday and why is EBRPD implementing it? ›
Workday is a cloud-based platform that will replace our current separate HR and Finance systems with one integrated solution. It will cover payroll, benefits, time tracking, recruiting, purchasing, and financial management — giving staff a single, modern system for people and money-related tasks.
What does this mean for my day-to-day work? ›
Most staff will use Workday to manage timecards, request time off, view pay stubs, and update personal information — all in one place. Finance and HR staff will have more significant workflow changes and will receive targeted training well before go-live.
When is the go-live date? ›
The full-suite go-live is targeted for 2027. The implementation is structured in phases and key milestone dates will be communicated on this page as they are confirmed. Nothing will change for staff without advance notice and training.
Do I need to do anything right now? ›
For most staff, no immediate action is required beyond staying informed. If your team has a specific request — such as a data review or survey — you will hear directly from HR or IT. Check the Action Items section above for the current status.
Who do I contact with questions? ›
For general questions, reach out to HR Training or Communications. For IT-related questions, contact the IT Application team. You can also submit a question using the form in the right panel and it will be routed to the right person.
